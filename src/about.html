    About Us

    Club Purpose

    The purpose of the machine learning club is to harness students curiosity about artificial intelligence and teach them valuable programming skills that they can apply in whatever field they choose to pursue in life. Machine learning is on the rise, and is being applied to cutting edge research and industry. Bronx Science has a strong focus on STEM, and machine learning is an increasingly important part of a good STEM education. The machine learning club will strengthen students coding abilities as well as their math skills, increasing their chances of success in advanced STEM courses. Most importantly the ML club will teach students how to think critically about a variety of problems, igniting their curiosity and passion for science.
