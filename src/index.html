Welcome to Bronx Science Machine Learning Club!

We are a nonprofit club at Bronx Science with the goal of introducing students to Machine Learning and enabling them to solve real-world problems through our carefully thought out projects.


We hope to inspire other students to think critically and develop strong problem solving skills. We hope to create the next generation of STEM leaders.
